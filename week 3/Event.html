MUSEUM OF ICE CREAM

#ABOUT

Remember those crazy ideas you dreamed up as a kid? The museum of ice-cream is the place where ideas are transformed into real life experiences a place where flavors are mysteries, toppings are toys and sprinkles make the world a better place. Our mission is to design environments that bring people together and provoke imagination.
